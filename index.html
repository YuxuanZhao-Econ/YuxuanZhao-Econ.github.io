Yuxuan Zhao

I am a first-year Ph.D. student in Economics at the University of Minnesota. My research interests are in macroeconomics, behavioral economics, and macro-finance. Broadly speaking, my research focuses on the role of imperfect information in macroeconomic business cycles. I am also interested in heterogeneous-agent models and the informational effects of public signals.
